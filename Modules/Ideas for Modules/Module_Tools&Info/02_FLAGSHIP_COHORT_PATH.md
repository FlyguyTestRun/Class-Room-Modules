# CoreSkills4AI — Flagship Cohort Path

## Cohort Name
Foundations → Agentic Pro

---

## Phase 1 — AI Systems Foundations
Duration: 4–6 weeks

Modules:
- A1. AI Systems Mental Models
- A2. Failure Modes & Anti-Patterns
- B1. Context Is the Product

Milestone:
Explain *why* systems fail before building anything complex.

---

## Phase 2 — Context & Retrieval Engineering
Duration: 4–6 weeks

Modules:
- B2. RAG Systems
- B3. Hybrid Retrieval
- Capstone 1: Controlled RAG System

Milestone:
Students build a cost-aware, explainable RAG system.

---

## Phase 3 — Agentic Systems Engineering
Duration: 6–8 weeks

Modules:
- C1. Agent Boundaries & Control
- C2. Multi-Agent Patterns
- C3. Observability

Capstone 2:
Agent system with explicit guardrails and telemetry.

---

## Phase 4 — Production Reality
Duration: 4–6 weeks

Modules:
- D1. AI Cost Engineering
- D2. Governance-by-Design
- D3. Hostile Environments

Final Capstone:
Enterprise-grade, governed multi-agent workflow.
